ALTAS' John Abis joins PBA Rookie Draft

Perpetual ALTAS Men's Basketball Team's John Cedric Abis has applied for the PBA Draft Combine, which will happen on September 4 and 5, 2025, at the Ynares Sports Arena in Pasig. Abis plays the position of Power Forward for the ALTAS. Following the Rookie Draft, he was included in the list of 128 applicants from various universities, including De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde, Colegio de San Juan de Letran, and Arellano University. The Season 50 Rookie Draft will be held on September 7, at 5:00 p.m., at the SM Mall of Asia Music Hall.
